Privacy Policy

Lead Tracker — a product of Vacations by Lyssa LLC
Effective Date: March 1, 2026 | Last Updated: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Vacations by Lyssa LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use Lead Tracker ("the App"), available at app.bdevtravelcollective.com.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

1. Who We Are

Lead Tracker is a subscription-based software tool created for independent travel advisors. It is operated by Lyssa, an independent travel agent under Vacations by Lyssa LLC. For questions about this policy, contact us at [email protected].

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, full name, and agency name.

Lead Data: The App allows you to store information about your travel leads including names, email addresses, phone numbers, vacation preferences, travel dates, notes, and communication history. This data belongs to you and is stored securely in our database.

Notes: Any notes you create manually or save from AI-generated content are stored and associated with your account.

Payment Information: Subscription payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number or payment details. Stripe's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment data and can be found at stripe.com/privacy.

Usage Data: We may collect basic usage information such as login timestamps and feature usage to improve the App. We do not sell this data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • To process your subscription and send billing-related communications
  • To respond to support requests sent to [email protected]
  • To send important notices about the App (such as changes to this policy or service outages)
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

We do not sell your personal information or your leads data to any third party. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

4. AI Features and Third-Party Processing

The App includes AI-powered features ("Atlas") that use the Anthropic Claude API to generate email drafts, analyze messages, and suggest follow-up content. When you use these features, the content you submit (such as pasted client messages or conversation threads) is sent to Anthropic's API for processing.

You should not submit sensitive personal information — such as social security numbers, financial account numbers, or health information — through the AI features. Anthropic's privacy policy governs how they handle API data and can be found at anthropic.com/privacy.

AI-generated content is provided as a suggestion only. You are responsible for reviewing and editing all AI-generated communications before sending them to clients.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Supabase, a managed database platform with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. Each user's data is isolated using row-level security, meaning no user can access another user's leads or notes.

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and lead data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription, your data remains stored and accessible if you resubscribe. If you request account deletion, we will delete your data within 30 days. To request deletion, email [email protected].

7. Cookies

The App uses session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your lead data (contact support for assistance)

To exercise any of these rights, email [email protected].
